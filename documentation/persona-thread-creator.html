Interaction Flow for posting content on Rethreads

Use Case

Brooke wants to post a thread to promote her blog post about her recent trip to the Pacific Northwest.

Precondition

Brooke has signed up for Rethreads and is viewing the homepage in her browser.

Post-condition

Brook has posted a thread

  • Rethreads shows the homepage including a navbar with a sign in link.
  • Brooke clicks the sign in link.
  • Rethreads displays a modal with a sign in form.
  • Brooke enters her email address and password and clicks the submit button to sign in.
  • Rethreads displays Brooke's feed of current threads and a form to submit a thread.
  • Brooke types in a short teaser about her blog post and adds a picture, then clicks submit.
  • Rethreads adds Brooke's new thread to her and her followers feed.
  • Brooke sees her new thread in her feed.
